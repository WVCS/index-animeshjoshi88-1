Players Accolades
Nero Hamidi Nero Hamidi is the starting point guard for Mercury Hoops. He stands at 5 feet 9 inches and is known for his perimeter shooting and playmaking. Nero Hamidi averages 17.6 Points Per Game, 4.2 Rebounds, 6.3 Assists and 1.3 steals.
Animesh Joshi Animesh Joshi is the starting shooting guard for Mercury Hoops. He stands at 5 feet and 10 inches, and is known for his finishing and mid range shooting. This season, Animesh is averaging 15.2 Points Per Game, 6.2 Rebounds, 2.6 Assists, and 1.5 steals.
Anirudh Kannan (Amutha) Anirudh Kannan is the starting small forward for Mercury Hoops. He stands at 5 feet 8 inches, and is known for his elite isolation game and his fighting spirit. This season Anirudh is averaging 6.4 Points Per Game, 8.6 Rebounds, 0.2 Assists, 3.4 steals, and 5.7 turnovers, all while shooting a blazing 12% from the field. He is widely considered to be the MVP of this team.
Brandon Le Brandon Le is the starting power forward for mercury hoops. He stands at 5 feet 9 inches and is known for his physical play and clutch shooting. This season, Brandon is averaging 5.7 points per game, 9.8 rebounds per game, 2.5 assists per game, and 2.2 steals per game.
Sean Le Sean Le is the starting center for mercury hoops. He stands at 5 feet 7 inches and is known for his rebounding tenacity and cuts to the basket. This season, Sean is averaging 6.6 points per game, 14.8 rebounds per game, 1.2 assists per game, and 3.4 blocks per game.
Eli Tolentino (Spaghetti) Eli Tolentino is the sixth man for mercury hoops. He stands at 5 feet 11 inches and is known for his physical play and world class defense. This season, Eli is averaging 6.2 points per game, 11.8 rebounds per game, 3.5 assists per game, 2.9 steals per game, and 6 personal fouls. Eli would be a no brainer for the starting lineup, however due to immense pressure from his employers at Rubios he is unable to make an appearance on multiple occasions.
